DOTS rules

Dots & Boxes
How to playQuick

Players take turns drawing one line between two adjacent dots (horizontal or vertical).

If you complete the fourth side of a square (a “box”), you claim that box and score 1 point, and you get to go again.

When all possible lines are drawn, the match ends. Highest score wins.

Good strategyOptional

Avoid giving your opponent the “third side” of a box. Late game often becomes a chain where one player can capture multiple boxes in a row.
